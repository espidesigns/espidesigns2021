About

I’m Mark Louie Espedido, a Creative Developer based in Singapore, specialized in Corporate Branding, and Builder of Websites and Apps that work decently well on nearly every kind of web-enabled device.

"Espi" was my nickname since school. It came from my last name. When teachers ask other students if I do design, my friends would answer, "Yes, Espi designs." I have used my brand name since school and it still sticks to me until now.

I’m working at Diners Club Singapore as a Senior Web Developer/UIUX Designer. In the past, I’ve also collaborated with other companies and freelancers.
